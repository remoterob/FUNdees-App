Medical Declaration

Freediving is a strenuous activity with inherent risks. Please confirm none of the following apply to you, or that you have sought medical clearance if they do:

☐ Regular medication (prescribed or over-the-counter)
☐ Mental illness, mood disorder, or psychiatric condition
☐ Neurological conditions — seizures, stroke, blackouts, severe migraines
☐ Cardiovascular conditions — heart attack, irregular heartbeat, uncontrolled high blood pressure
☐ Pulmonary conditions — asthma, collapsed lung, emphysema, breathing problems
☐ Ear, nose or throat conditions — sinus surgery, ruptured eardrum, persistent sinus infection
☐ Eye conditions — severe myopia, retinal detachment, eye surgery
☐ Diabetes requiring insulin or oral medication
☐ Previous freediving/scuba incident — blackout, decompression sickness, lung squeeze
☐ Currently pregnant
